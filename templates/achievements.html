1. The Proposal

Inspired by the impactful mission of "Như chưa hề có cuộc chia ly", I developed an AI-powered solution to reunite families as my graduation project. The project, titled "Application of Artificial Intelligence in Supporting the Search and Identification of Missing Relatives," was born from a desire to apply technology to a deeply human cause.

Continuous communication and collaboration with the program's technical team.

2. The Collaboration

My work earned the highest score (9.1/10) from the evaluation council and received glowing reviews from industry experts. This recognition led to a prestigious invitation to collaborate with the technical team of "Như chưa hề có cuộc chia ly" and its celebrated founder, journalist Thu Uyên. In my role as a Data Scientist, I designed the core RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system for matching records.

The final product - a web portal for finding missing persons.

3. The Result

The collaboration resulted in a powerful, user-friendly web portal that leverages advanced AI to analyze and match missing person records. By implementing Semantic Search and Vector Search, the system automates the matching process, significantly reducing the time and effort required to bring families back together.
